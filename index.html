The standard EdGE pre-departure course is 12 units long. We begin with reflection on our own intentions and assumptions, and then we examine problematic trends in international volunteering and within the broader landscape of aid and 'development.'

Then we explore inequality from various angles before digging deep into the buzzy concepts of 'culture' and 'community.' Finally, we end on the topic of storytelling. How can you use an international field position as a platform to tell a 'polyvocal' story?

Dedicated One-on-One Mentorship

You pick an EdGE mentor who works with you in the pre-departure classroom, provides guidance as you create your Capstone, and supports you once you come back home. EdGE mentors draw from their professional and academic expertise to provide you with personalized insight and support before, during, and after your international experience.

Meet the entire EdGE Team

Capstone Projects

EdGE pre-departure training provides you with the tools and structure for conducting an empirical inquiry about a social issue that is important to you and your host. During your field position, you will build a digital archive — a 'Record of Perspectives' — and then you will synthesize this work into a Capstone Project to be published and shared throughout our network.

EdGE training and mentorship is a perfect complement to self-directed international experiences.

Authentic educational and logistical support

Volunteers and interns receive personalized support with finding a field position in our network (if desired), dealing with logistics like flights and visas, and grappling with the ethical complexities of trying to ‘serve others’ when crossing significant differences of culture and power. We accept applications on a rolling basis, so join us anytime!

Ethics and affordability go hand in hand

The tuition for the standard EdGE program is $750. There are no additional fees if you choose to work with one of our Partners. Participants are responsible for their own airfare and living expenses while abroad, and our Partners aren't trying to profit off of you (or they wouldn't be Partners!). Most Partners can offer food and accommodations for less than $15 per day. Plus, EdGE students are eligible for the Omprakash Ambassador Travel Grant and can conduct crowdfunding campaigns on the Omprakash platform.
